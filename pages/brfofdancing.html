Kathak

Welcome to the Kathak Foundation Course where we introduce your child to one of India’s most revered classical dance forms - Kathak. Our course is meticulously crafted to ensure that the student builds a strong foundation - from simple footwork to expressive dance movements. Kathak is one of the nine major forms of Indian classical dance. Its origin is attributed to the traveling bards in ancient northern India known as Kathakar ("storyteller"), who communicated stories from the Hindu epics through dance, songs and music. Its name derives from the Sanskrit word katha

Bharatanatyam

Bharatanatyam is a classical dance from South India that is over 2,000 years old. It is known for its intricate footwork, facial expressions, and hand gestures. Bharatanātyam is an Indian classical dance form that originated in Tamil Nadu, India. It is a classical dance form recognized by the Sangeet Natak Akademi, and expresses South Indian religious themes and spiritual ideas of Hinduism and Jainism.

Bollywood Dance

Bollywood dance is a vibrant, energetic style that blends traditional Indian dance with modern moves and Western influences. It's known for its dramatic facial expressions, lively footwork, and colorful costumes. Bollywood Dance style is a fusion of various dance styles. It includes Bharatanatyam, Kathak, Bhangra, Jazz, Hip-hop, Arabic, Western dance to name a few. Classical Indian dance incorporates the two basic elements of dance and expression.

Hip-Hop

Hip hop dance is a style of street dance form that evolved from hip hop culture and hip hop music. It borrows elements from a number of different styles like African dance, tap, and ballet. Hip hop dance began as a freestyle dance performed in the streets by dance crews, mostly to hip hop music. Hip-hop is a subculture defined by four key stylistic elements: MCing/rapping, DJing/scratching with turntables, breakdancing, and graffiti art or writing. Knowledge is sometimes described as a fifth element, underscoring its role in shaping the values and promoting empowerment and consciousness-raising through music.
